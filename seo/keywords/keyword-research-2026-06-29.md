## Headline takeaways

- **Best theme:** "Click & Collect restaurant" + the **0%-commission** angle. The SERP for
  `click and collect restaurant` is wall-to-wall direct competitor SaaS landing pages — several
  using Piki's exact "sans commission" hook (Tastycloud, Collectly, Delicity) — proving a dedicated
  landing page can rank and the positioning works.
- **No head term to win.** French restaurant-tech volumes are low; the play is owning a *cluster*
  of low-volume, high-intent terms + comparison/guide content, not one big keyword.
- **Don't chase `click and collect` (9,900):** it's navigational/B2C retail (shoppers, Auchan), not
  a B2B fit.
